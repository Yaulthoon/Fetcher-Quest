A Kind of Slush

Oh my, it seems that the bridge has caved in from the weight of the snow from the latest blizzard. There's no way across for miles and you must find your way across the lake. Your only option is to hop across the larger sheets of ice to the other side, but be careful not to trap yourself while doing so.

Each jump you make causes the larger blocks of ice to sink below the surface while the smaller blocks rise to the surface. Think carefully before making each move or you may find yourself stranded in the middle of the lake!

Objective: Start from the bottom of the lake and make it to the bridge at the opposite end.
