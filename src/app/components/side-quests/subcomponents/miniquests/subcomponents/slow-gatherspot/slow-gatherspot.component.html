Gathering Spot

As you are traveling, you take the time to view your surroundings carefully for both danger and opportunity alike. In this case, you find a small mine nearby. Looking at some of the minecarts at the entrance, you see that this mine likely contains a generous supply of coal. It seems abandoned at the moment and you could probably venture in to gather some ore to sell to a merchant along the way.

You figure as long as you do not stay long, you will not suffer any ill-effects from the mining excursion itself. Knowing when to call it quits would be the trick.

As you continue to gather ore, you hear a low growl come from the entrance of the cave. As you squint to adjust your eyes to the brightness of the cave entrance, you realize that this must be the dwelling of a catbear! Half cat, half bear, a quarter cute, three-quarters dangerous, all catbear.

You have heard about catbears before from other adventurers. You could try to make yourself appear large by throwing out your arms and scaring the catbear, but there is no guarantee it would not attack out of fear rather than run away. Contrary to popular opinion on the matter, staying perfectly still will only get you mauled by the catbear. They have an excellent sense of smell and can detect you easily even in the shadows. The best course of action would be to overpower its senses with a pungent odor...like human urine.

Knowing that bears naturally fear humans, you decide to stay still in the hopes that once it notices you, the catbear will flee the cave allowing you to leave safely. On the contrary, the catbear appears to turning up its nose high into the air. It must smell you as it closes in on your position. Can it not see you due to the darkness of the cave? It's getting closer now. Very close. You could change course if you acted right away.

The catbear begins to realize what you are, and feeling threatened, immediately starts to attack you. You take two damage right away and now must attempt to flee with your live. You really only have two options available to you at this point.

You struggle intensely to free yourself from the beast. Through some miracle, it raises up to finish you off and hits the back of its head against the ceiling of the cave. Capitalizing on the opportunity, you crawl between its legs and run out of the cave as fast as you can. After making it a safe distance away, you can start to feel the effects of your skirmish and the resulting injuries. Opting to play dead feels like a mistake as the catbear continues to have his way with you for what feels like an eternity. Eventually, the creature feels that you are satisfactorily deceased and leaves the cave to continue its wandering. You wait some time to make sure it does not return and leave the cave carefully.
