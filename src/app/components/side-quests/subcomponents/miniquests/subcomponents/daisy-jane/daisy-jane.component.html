"Please good sir you must help me! You get the feeling this could be a crazy man playing his crazy game. At the same time, you would hate to leave this man and his, well, whatever this Daisy is to him, to brave the woods at night with no shelter or supplies.

That being said, you have seen this sort of behavior before. You see this ending one of two ways: either this hapless man is some deranged stalker and you will eventually have to subdue him or this man has gotten himself involved with something far more sinister than he realizes...

You notice on one of the nearby trees that someone has etched the sign for extreme danger. For such a peaceful looking forest to contain such a warning, you wonder what kind of threat lies within the woods. You should be on your guard.

That being said, you hesitantly agree to help the man in finding his Daisy Jane.
