Wellfare

In your travels you come across a well just off the roadside. Feeling thirsty, you decide to stop to fill your waterskin. As you approach the well, you see a number of gold coins on its rim. Is this supposed to be some sort of wishing well? You could make a donation for good luck, but then again, you could just take the coins yourself since no one else is around to judge you.

As you examine the well and consider your options, you take notice that the coins on the well emit an ominous aura. You did not notice at first glance, but these coins are clearly cursed. Taking them would be an illfated idea.

{{this.wellWishes[this.times]}}

You have thrown a ludricrous amount of gold into this well hoping for...well, I am not exactly sure what you were hoping for. Wait a second, you hear a rumbling within the well. Suddenly, water shoots out accompanied by an absurd amount of gold. Taking the time to collect it, you realize that all {{this.donations}} of the gold you tossed in has appeared before you. Also, you find a few artifacts that appear to be valuable as well. You gratefully store them in your treasure bag to sell later.

Is there some sort of moral lesson here? Maybe...it pays to throw gold around in situations that do not warrant it? Its hard to say.

After counting it up, you pocketed a total of 20 gold for your efforts. As you continue along your way, you start to feel a little unwell. You wonder if getting sick was a coincidence or is karma catching up with you? Its hard to say.

While you respect the wishes of others to believe in superstitions, you see no gain from throwing money in yourself, nor do you wish to take from the good will of others. You ignore the well and carry on.

You donated to the well, and while the breeze was nice, that's all it was. Not feeling inclined to cast any more money into the well, you continue on your journey. Maybe the good fortune will help you in your travels. Its hard to say.
