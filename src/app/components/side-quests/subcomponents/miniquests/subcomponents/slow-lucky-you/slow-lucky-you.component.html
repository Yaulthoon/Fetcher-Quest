Lucky You

Meandering along at a casual pace, you notice a small, covered mound on the ground just off the road. Investigating further, you realize the mound is a small cache of trade goods wrapped in cloth. It must have fallen off a cart of some busy merchant speeding down the path. It is clear that these goods have been sitting on the ground for a while, so they are not in pristine condition.

Regardless, you figure this is about 100 gold worth of trade goods that you can easily sell on your next visit to the merchant. Sometimes it pays to take the slow and steady path.
