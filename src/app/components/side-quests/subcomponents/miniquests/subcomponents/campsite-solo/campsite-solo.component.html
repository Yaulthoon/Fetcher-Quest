A Rare Respite

As the sun begins to dip below the horizon and the last light of the day begins to fade into obscurity, you find a nice clearing that would make an ideal campsite for the night. This must one of the designated campsites marked on your map. You are sure resting here would bring no danger. You consider if you need to rest or not given your condition. It will cost you 1 supply to camp which will recover some Hp. You decide to:

You decide that you are feeling pretty energetic, or miserly, or some variation of the two. Either way, you forgo the opportunity to camp and continue onward one supply richer.

Not one to look a gift horse in the mouth, you lay out your supplies and set camp for the night. As you expected, no complications occur through the night and you are able to recover some of your lost stamina. You heal for 3 Hp. Feeling invigorated, you gather your belongings and set off in the morning sun.
