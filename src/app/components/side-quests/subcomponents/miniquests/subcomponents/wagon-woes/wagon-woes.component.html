Wagon Woes

With how hard you've been traveling so far, it was bound to happen. One of your wagon wheels finally gave way and now you're stuck in the middle of the road. You could probably patch the wheel up with some of your various supplies quickly if you have some to spare. If not, you might be stuck here until a kind traveler comes by and helps you out which could take a while.

You spend a little time mending your wagon wheel and its good as new. It may have costed you more supplies than you would have liked, but it is the cost of the daily grind after all. Thankfully, you are able to increase your pace a bit, so you do not lose any distance in your day's travel.

After quite some time, you can see a merchant wagon traveling along the path. You hail the individual and explain the situation. The merchant seems to feel for your plight and is willing to make a deal, for a price.

"That's quite a pickle to be in. I know how it is to get stranded on the road. I don't have any wheels for sell unfortunately. Well, tell you what, I can sell you my own spare wheel if you want. Considering the risk I will be undertaking in traveling without a spare myself, I suppose I could sell it to you for 100 gold?"

Buying the wheel will let you get back on the road quickly and will allow you to make up most, but not all, of your day's expected travel distance. You decide to:

Feeling a little extorted, you agree to purchase the merchant's wheel. Although the wheel was expensive, the merchant at least had the kindness to help you mount the wheel before he left. At least you can continue your travels. Despite losing a little time, you are able to largely salvage your day's travel.

After another several hours, you find a traveler willing to help you with your plight without attempting to price gouge you. Kind strangers still do exist apparently. Although you were able to get your wagon fixed free of charge, you lost nearly the entire's day worth of travel due to the long wait for help. Perhaps next time you will carry some extra supplies with you for this sort of situation?
