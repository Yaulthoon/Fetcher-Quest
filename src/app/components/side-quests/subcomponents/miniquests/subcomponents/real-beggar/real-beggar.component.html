Alms For The Poor

You come across a beggar along the road. Although he has his hood up due to the rain, his shabby cloak only covers about half of his body. He stands up in order to greet you.

Hello good sir. I don't suppose you can spare some gold for a man down on his luck could you? Whatever you can spare would be most appreciated, I have a lot of mouths to feed and not enough gold. Our crops have done poorly this year and I do not know if we can make it through winter.

You know there are a lot of beggars along these roads; not all of them truthful. Some would only use the gold you give them to fund their drug habits. Still, knowing this, you decide to:

Grateful for your generosity, the beggar removes his hood and shakes your hand heartily. He explains that he is a priest that travels from town to town providing for the poor. The priest uses the gold from travelers to purchase spell components for his healing spells. The amount that you gave him should go a great way towards buying more components. As you nod and turn to walk away, you feel a sudden warmth inside you as you heal for 5 Hp.

Grateful for your generosity, the beggar removes his hood and shakes your hand heartily. He explains that he is a priest that travels from town to town providing for the poor. The priest uses the supplies from travelers to feed and bandage the needy who cannot afford a proper doctor. The amount that you gave him should provide a great deal of food for the nearby farmers that have lost much of their harvest this year. As you nod and turn to walk away, the priest gives you 3 bandages to show his thanks. He tells you that he is adept at making them from scraps of cloth and assures you the supplies you gave him will allow him to make plenty more.

Maybe you did not trust the beggar. Or maybe you did not have anything to spare. Regardless, you opt to give him nothing and continue on your journey.
