A Storm A-Brewin'

You smell it. A storm a-brewin'. Your tumbleweed yearns to roll across the road in an out of control manner. You could try to keep traveling at your current pace, but doing so is risky. You see a small cave nearby that would make a natural shelter against the storm. Waiting there until the storm passes would ensure your safety, but who knows how much progress you would lose by waiting instead of venturing forth?

You realize this is no ordinary storm approaching and seek shelter within the cave. As the rain begins to pummel against the sides of the cave, you feel a sense of relief that you did not risk traveling onward. A hour seems to pass without any signs of relief. Another hour passes and begin to wonder if the storm will ever let up.

You begin to feel a little stir-crazy when a flash of lightning lights up the cave. You hadn't noticed until just now, but there appear to be some mushrooms growing in this cave. You think to yourself: should I eat these?

Inspecting them closer, you recognize them as poisonous mushrooms with hallucinogenic properties. Who knows what disaster could await you if you were to consume them.

Under much consideration, you decide not to eat the mushrooms. Eventually, the storm passes and you exit the cave unharmed. Unfortunately, you ended up losing any progress that you would have made on this day's travels, but that is the price you pay sometimes for a safe voyage.

You begin to munch on the mushrooms without a second thought when you suddenly feel a strange sensation come over you.

These were poisonous mushrooms and you can tell that they would drive most people into a deranged psychosis, but for you, they do little more than lift your spirits. You actually feel energized from the meal. In fact, you gather the rest of them up to eat as snacks during your journey. Even though you lost out on your progress for the day, you managed to heal for 2 Hp and gain 2 supplies as you waited out the storm.

You are not sure what just happened. It could have just been the day's fatigue catching up with you. After you have your fill, you turn around to sit by the cave's entrance when you see several snakes creeping towards you. Did you rest in a den of snakes? My god, they are even crawling along the walls towards you! They look like they intend to do you harm! All your supplies are in the wagon outside. Equipped only with your fists, you decide to:

You decide to stay perfectly still and hope they ignore you. Nervous, you close your eyes and try to calm your breathing. Darkness comes. When you wake up, you do not see any snakes around you and the storm has passed. You feel weak and notice you have a touch of a fever. Was it the mushrooms? You are probably lucky that you are walking away only slightly worse for wear. You gather yourself up and continue on your journey.

As you begin to punch and kick your way through, you feel the sharp, unforgiving cave wall meet with your fist. Yeowch! You take 1 Hp of damage as you hurt your fist punching at the snakes. As you come to your senses, you realize that the snakes must have been a figment of your imagination. You sit back down in a groggy state and try to wait out the ill-effects of the mushrooms as the storm passes. Ultimately, the storm does pass and you can continue on your journey still feeling a little sick from the effects of the mushrooms.

Confident that no harm shall come your way as long as your faith holds strong, you press onward. Indeed, you can feel a warm presence attempting to protect you from this terrible storm.
