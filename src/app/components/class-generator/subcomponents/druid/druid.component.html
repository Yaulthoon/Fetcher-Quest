Druid

Class Perks

Passive: Nature's Ally

You may not have an interest in the life of your fellow man, but you share a unique bond with mother nature. During a rest, you will always heal for the full amount when camping on your own. Conversely, always heal for the minimum amount if camping with a group of people.

Active: A Weed in Need

Cost: 1HP. Consult with the locale flora and fauna. Doing so may grant you insight into potential dangers ahead or provide clues as to how to proceed in the current chapter.
