Class Perks

Passive: Blood Rush

You cannot stay still. To rest is to provide an opportunity for your enemies to plot their next move. Choosing the rush movement option requires only 2HP instead of the normal 3HP cost.

Active: A Tumbled Mind

Cost: 1HP. Untrusting and uncontrollable, you do not always see a reasonable solution to the problems you face. Activate this skill to see the one "sane" solution. Results may vary.
