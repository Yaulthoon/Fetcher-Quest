Paladin

Class Perks

Passive: Detect Goodies

You know a truly kind soul when you see one. If someone is of similar alignment to you, you will know it right away. You will come to no harm in your interactions with them.

Active: Lay On Hands

Cost: 3HP. Once per chapter, you may cast this divine spell upon yourself to heal for a fixed amount. The amount healed depends upon the following formula: (Your current order + Your current faith)/2 rounded up.
