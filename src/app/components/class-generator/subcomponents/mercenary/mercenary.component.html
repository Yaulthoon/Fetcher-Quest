Mercenary

Class Perks

Passive: Lay of the Land

As a mercenary, you are used to living off the land using the bare minimum to survive. When you choose the careful movement option, you use 2 supplies instead of the usual 3.

Active: First Blood

Cost: 2HP. Whenever you accept someone's request during a chapter, you may charge double the initial fee. You must initiate this skill prior to accepting the individual's request.
