Tumblemancer

Class Perks

Passive: In the Thicket of It

Given your experiences in the ways of tumblemancy, you possess immense knowledge of plant life. You can sometimes salvage useful supplies or detect the presence of intelligent plant life during your travels.

Active: Toil and Tumble

Cost: 2HP. You can summon a tumbleman to serve you. For all purposes, it will serve as your scout taking all damage in your stead should you come to harm. Tumbleman possesses 5HP and will disintegrate upon death or upon contact with fire. Some characters with strong faith may turn hostile and destroy your conjured creature if they discover what it is.
