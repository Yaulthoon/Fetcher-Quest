Rogue

Class Perks

Passive: Rogues' Cant

You are familiar with the secret symbology and language of your fellow thieves, beggars, and unscrupulous adventurers. You can pick up on signs of danger or refuge that normal people cannot.

Active: Rolling in the Weeds

Cost: 2HP. Sensing that danger may be imminent, you can use your tumbleweed to roll past inopportune situations. Skip the entire chapter you are currently on.
